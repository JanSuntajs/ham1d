Define the model constants¶

For now, we resort to those used in the paper. With $L_\mathrm{b}$, we denote the bath length, while $L_\mathrm{loc}$ denotes the length of the localized portion of the system. Disorder strength parameter is denoted, as usually, by $W$ and $g_0$ and $\alpha$ specify the couplings of LIOMs to the bath, respectively. Bath parameters are controlled by setting the value of $\beta$. We also provide a brief explanation in the comments.
